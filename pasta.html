Truffle pasta recipe

Description

This black truffle tagliatelle is a simple yet luxurious Italian pasta dish, where freshly cooked egg tagliatelle is tossed in a light buttery sauce and finished with generously shaved black truffle. The earthiness of the truffle is the star of the dish, so the sauce is intentionally minimal to let it shine.

The key to this dish is quality ingredients — fresh pasta and a good black truffle make all the difference. It comes together in minutes, making it an elegant option for a special dinner without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 300g fresh tagliatelle
  • 1 small black truffle
  • 60g unsalted butter
  • 50ml pasta cooking water
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Parmesan, freshly grated, to serve (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the tagliatelle according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 50ml of pasta cooking water before draining.
  2. While the pasta cooks, melt the butter in a large pan over low heat. Add a splash of pasta water and stir to emulsify into a light sauce.
  3. Add the drained tagliatelle to the pan and toss well to coat every strand in the butter sauce. Add more pasta water if needed to loosen.
  4. Season generously with salt and freshly cracked black pepper.
  5. Plate the pasta and use a mandoline or truffle shaver to shave the black truffle generously over the top.
  6. Serve immediately while warm.
