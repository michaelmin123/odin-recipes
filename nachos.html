Nachos recipe

Description

These loaded skillet nachos are piled high with crispy tortilla chips, layered with salsa and melted cheddar cheese, then finished with fresh spring onion and cilantro. Baking them in a cast iron skillet gives the bottom layer an irresistible crunch while the cheese on top becomes perfectly golden and gooey.

This is the ultimate sharing dish — generous, indulgent, and ready in under 20 minutes. Serve straight from the skillet for maximum effect.

Ingredients

  • 200g tortilla chips
  • 150g cheddar cheese, grated
  • 150g salsa
  • 3 spring onions, finely sliced
  • Small handful of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (180°C fan).
  2. Spread half the tortilla chips in an even layer across a cast iron skillet.
  3. Spoon half the salsa over the chips and scatter over half the grated cheddar.
  4. Add the remaining chips on top and repeat with the rest of the salsa and cheese.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10–12 minutes until the cheese is fully melted and bubbling.
  6. Remove from the oven and immediately top with sliced spring onion and fresh cilantro.
  7. Serve straight from the skillet.
